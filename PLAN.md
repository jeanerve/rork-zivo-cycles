# Zivo — Social Savings & Discipline Platform

## Features

- **Dashboard** — welcome message, total savings, AI insight card, growth graph with touch interaction, active cycle cards with animated progress bars, discipline streak, community challenges entry point
- **Live Growth Graph** — interactive graph on the homepage that tracks personal contributions over time, shows deposits (green) and withdrawals (red), touch to inspect data points with exact amount and date
- **4 Cycle Types** — Individual, Family, Community, and Teen — each with unique characteristics (member lists, leaderboards, parental controls, etc.)
- **Cycle Creation Flow** — purpose-driven creation with "What are you saving for?" and "Why does it matter?", step-by-step screens for type, goal, duration, frequency, and member invites
- **Cycle Detail Screen** — progress, contribution history, member list/leaderboard, growth chart, and **Cycle Health Score** out of 100 based on on-time payments, streak, missed contributions, and goal completion
- **Payment System** — grace periods (3 extra days), partial payment tracking, catch-up mechanism, payment schedule with due dates
- **Quick Contribution & Withdrawal** — add or withdraw money with preset amounts, haptic feedback, and graph animation
- **Discipline System** — streak tracking, discipline score (0-100%), detailed breakdown (on-time, late, missed, partial), milestone badges
- **After-Cycle Achievement Posts** — share what you achieved with photos, title, and story after completing a cycle
- **Zivo Feed (Community tab)** — browse achievement posts from other users, like and comment, positive and motivational atmosphere
- **Community Challenges** — join challenges like No-Spend Week, $5 Daily, Student Saver, Emergency Fund Sprint; track progress; verify on leaderboards
- **Challenge Dashboard** — see all challenge members, profile pictures, streaks, amounts saved, leaderboard ranking, days remaining, rules, and pot size; NO DMs or chat (accountability, not another social app)
- **Social Boost** — when someone hits a milestone, friends can boost them with +$1, +$5, or +$10 that goes directly into their savings
- **Verification Badges** — Verified Identity (government ID), Verified Bank (connected account), Verified Saver (3+ cycles), Elite Discipline (10+ cycles); displayed on profile, leaderboards, and feed
- **Profile Screen** — avatar, total saved, active cycles, streak, discipline breakdown, 3 achievements with progress, verification badges, Zivo virtual card
- **Zivo AI Assistant** — personalized saving insights, motivation, and analysis (accessible from Settings, stays out of the main experience)
- **Virtual Card** — custom-designed Zivo debit card with lock/unlock, card number regeneration (new number + CVV instantly), Apple Pay/Google Pay toggle, card design customization (6 themes, 5 patterns)
- **Spending Categories** — 7 categories (Food, Shopping, Entertainment, Lifestyle, Gaming, Transport, Other); set monthly budgets; auto-lock when budget reached; toggle individual category locks
- **Round Ups 2.0** — automatically round up spare change from purchases; direct to active cycle, milestone goal, or general savings; track total rounded amount
- **Notifications** — in-app feed for reminders, contributions, milestones, completions, withdrawal requests

## Design

- **Dark theme** with near-black background (#050508) and vibrant green accents (#00E676)
- Premium glass-card style with subtle borders and depth
- Smooth animated progress bars with green glow effect
- Interactive growth graph with gradient fill, touch inspection, green dots for deposits, red for withdrawals
- Rounded avatars with green status rings for group members
- Large bold numbers for financial amounts, clean sans-serif typography
- Haptic feedback on all key interactions
- Subtle entrance animations for cards and list items
- Calm, modern, emotionally intelligent design — not a corporate banking app
